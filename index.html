About OGUN CDC

Ogun state is endowed with good and most intelligent people among all states in Nigeria, we are known for educational development and human coordination in terms of politics, ethnic friendly and social harmonization. Ogun state in its vast social development, increment and expansion in lands and housing in the last ten years has called for Ogun state government to guide against boundary adjustment in over seven thousand registered CDAs in the state, this will ensure peaceful coexistence among the CDAs across all the CDC Areas in Ogun state.

From our experience and findings, boundary adjustment has been one of the major crises among CDAs in Ogun state which sometimes lead to fracas and enmities between two or more communities. We have witnessed dozens of boundary adjustment dispute within and outside Abeokuta metropolis in the last few years, where most of the affected communities involved are living among themselves as cat and dog. If these situations persist, it could jeopardize the long built peaceful and harmony legacy of our fore fathers and draw us back for some decades to restructure it again.

Electronic Boundary Adjustment Application is a web and mobile base application which will allow state government and CDAs to know their boundary areas at their finger tips, each CDA members will have their community profiles and development status uploaded online for free access among members.

State government will have access to all CDAs activities through the portal, and have access to their various activities and their needs in the area, government can also disseminate information to all CDAs on the platform.

How it Works:

Each CDAs will submit the geographical boundary area to the state government for review, after passing the minimum requirement and settlement among bounded CDAs, Ogun state government will erect official sign post in the name of the community in conformity process that the boundary belongs to the applicant community, the sign post will carry names of the CDA and logo of Ogun state Government and certificate number of the CDA.

Each CDA will have login and password to view their community profile and status online, there will be public publications for every CDA.

This Private Portal will allow people to report malicious and sensitive issues to the state government, the portal will be strictly for the CDC chairmen and its executives, this will allow government to strengthen their security network in cooperation with security agencies across the state.
